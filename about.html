Meet The Team

Adam and Klaudia founded Etch after graduating from University of Commerce Dublin.

Upon witnessing the frustrations of others when using complex software applications or flimsy paper,
the co-founders saw a real need for long lasting mediums to capture human ingenuity with elegance and ease.

After three years of collaboration with leading researchers and manufacturers throughout Ireland,
the team is proud to announce the upcoming launch of EtchPad!
