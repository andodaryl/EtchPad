Adam and Klaudia founded Etch after graduating from University of Commerce Dublin.

The co-founders saw a real need for long lasting mediums to capture human ingenuity with elegance and ease.

The team endeavoured to create a product to alleviate the frustrations of using complex software applications or flimsy paper.

After three years of collaboration with leading researchers and manufacturers throughout Ireland, Etch is proud to announce the upcoming launch of EtchPad!
